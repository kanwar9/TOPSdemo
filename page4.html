The fastest and safest way to import and export goods internationally is by air. TOPS air cargo specialists will assess your specific needs and customize our air services for your large-scale project or smaller express shipment door to door, terminal to terminal, or anything in-between.


Global Cargo Movements

TK has been an IATA approved Cargo Agent ever since the entry of IATA into India in 1949. This has enabled TK to develop an extensive Worldwide Agency Network for air logistics, which enables it to provide total customer satisfaction in freight forwarding and Global logistics. TK offers Door - To - Door and Airport - To - Airport as well as Shipment Monitoring Services to exporters and importers throughout India.
