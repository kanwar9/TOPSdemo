ABOUT US


TOPS

Is One Of The Leading Customs Brokers Since 1987,

An Indian logistics company with a collective utilization of our worldwide network of offices and associates, we are well capable of meeting the diverse needs of today’s demanding business needs in the competitive markets.

We offer International Freight Forwarding, Transportation, Distribution, Insurance, Custom Clearance, Third Party Imports and Exports and EXIM consultancy services. We take pride to be a specialist in CUSTOMS BROKING.

Capacity, Capability and Connectivity is our strength which helps us to build ongoing relationships with our customers.

We have decades of experience in handling various types of Cargo through the major shipping lines and airlines. As one of the pioneers, we have paved a way in GLOBAL LOGISTICS with a record of over 30 years and still going strong.

We seek to differentiate ourselves by offering the most reliable, flexible and cost effective solutions to our customers. A benchmarked quality, standardized processes and operation excellence makes us win all our customers’ trust.

Our network is PAN India comprising of 12 branches covering key ports and cities.



TOPS is established as the leading Custom House Agents primarily focusing on Custom- Clearance, Shipping, Project-Forwarding, International Freight Forwarding, Warehousing, Exim Projects, ODC & Logistic Services in and around National Capital Region in India.

Tops have emerged as exclusive Core-Customs Brokers, Pioneers in serving major industrial plants and MNC’s. Along with being the early birds in establishing Multimodal Logistics & ICD Operations initially at Pragati Maidan (Earlier ICD of Northern Railway in mid 1980’s).

We Deal in Clearance of IMPORT and EXPORT Cargo and have real expertise in Import Clearance of Capital Goods, New and Used Machinery, Equipments, Parts, Electronic Components, Automobile Components, Finished Goods, CBU’s, Chemicals and other Raw Materials.

Tops is experienced, best equipped and clearing more than 1000 TEU’s per Month BY-SEA in ICD Tuglakabad-Delhi, ICD Patparganj-Delhi, ICD-Dadri Complex:-CFS Albatross, CFS Star Track Terminals & CFS CMA-CGM, ICD Garhi Hasrur Gurgaon, ICD Patli-Gurgaon, ICD Ballabhgarh- ACTL Faridabad and BY-AIR Regular Daily Basis Shipments at IGI Airport Cargo Terminal New Delhi.

We have been serving the Electronics, Automobile, Retail, Telecom and Textile Industry in northern India for over 30 Years and have been providing proficient speedy services to various leading MNC’s which include YKK India Pvt. Ltd., Panasonic India Ltd., Carrier Media India Pvt. Ltd., Maruchi-Kuma Stainless Tubes Ltd., STI Sanoh India Ltd., Samsung India Electronics Pvt. Ltd., LG Electronics India Pvt. Ltd, Sony India Ltd., Oberthur Technologies India Pvt. Ltd., Carrefour WC&C India Pvt. Ltd. etc.

We specialize in clearance of CTV, LCD, Refrigerator, Washing machine, Microwave Oven Window/Split Air-conditioned, CBU’s Finished goods, Raw material, Components, Parts, Machines Equipments, New & used Capital goods, Moulds, Dies, Steel Sheets, Zip Fasteners Shipments etc.
