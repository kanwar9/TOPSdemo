Handled & Cleared various PROJECTS IMPORT OF CAPITAL GOODS in the recent past. Tops are experienced, best equipped and clearing more than 1000 TEU’s per Month BY-SEA /AIR.

Professional Strength

Established in the year 1987, having Professional strength for the following areas such as Sea and Air Co-ordination, Office backup and documentation, TOPS has successfully pioneered concept of Inter modal services in Northern India. TOPS have various technical experts as associates & consultants. Staff Strength/Logistics Team: Approx 50 personals including professional/local handling teams.

COMPUTERISATION NETWORK

TOPS office is equipped with a fully fledged E.D.P Department and Custom Clearance documentation is completely computerized. The office is equally equipped with special In- House software to handle import and export and we also have E.D.I connection with Indian Customs.

COMMUNICATION NETWORK

A dedicated Account Manager, provided with Phones, Fax, e-mail and Internet is assigned to every client for better customer service. The field Staff is also provided with Mobile Phones for Instant/On-Spot information about all work progress. All the site offices and field staff are in constant touch with Head office from where the information is passed on to the clients consistently.

Customized Services & Daily Shipment Records

We Customize our clients specific requirements and provide daily Shipment wise, Container Wise Status to them with effective tracking with AIRLINES, SHIPPING LINES, AIRPORT, CFS/ICDs/Ports towards clearance status ,duty intimation, deposits, required documents, delivery etc..




CDocumentation – Fully Computerized Data Base

· Tops experienced team provides complete documentation services i.e. Documentation is maintained as – CLIENT WISE- JOB WISE

· A complete detail of materials/parts/components is maintained in computer as database with ITC HSN Classifications, Customs tariff Headings and Custom notifications along their with respective part numbers.

· Special In-House developed software facilitates to generate manual Bill of Entries covering up to 1000 different types of components.

· Full-time Connectivity with Indian Custom’s EDI ICEGATE System providing speedy clearance.

TRADE UPDATES

Tops provide updates to Clients on the ongoing Trade Developments and Notification / Circulars, Allied ACT etc. The ALLIED ACT becomes very important for the compulsory compliance at the time of Clearance.
