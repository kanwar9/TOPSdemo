CUSTOM
Clearance

As customs House Agents from 1987, TOPS has proven credentials both to importers and exporters. We are one of the oldest and leading Customs House Agents with an experience of 35 years. We provide Import and Export customs clearance services at all major air and sea ports in India.

With over three decades of operation & Handling expertise, we combine extensive customs clearing and forwarding experience with the latest in information technology.

We adhere to strict customs compliance and quality policy for all our operations; thereby eliminating delays and providing reliable service to our customers worldwide.


We are hands-on with the latest in customs compliances, regulations and procedures and offer clearance of import and export consignments by sea, air and road.

Handling Customs Clearance requires professional understanding of the subject, since the Laws and Procedures related to the Customs change from time to time based on trade requirements. We have a well trained and qualified team to handle all types of cargo and commodities.

Our comprehensive infrastructure facilities ensure efficient C&F Services. We are considered to be the most reliable CHA agents. Our aim is to deliver an efficient, professional, timely service, specific to your individual requirements.

Combining professional skills, decades of experience, and the resolution to excel, we endeavor to achieve total customer satisfaction by offering quality custom clearance services.

TOPS is routinely engaged by foreign consulates, trade missions as well as major international suppliers for import, EXPORT and re-export clearances of exhibition and project materials. Besides, TOPS is also proficient in custom clearance for Export Promotion Zones like SEEPZ, Software Technology Parks and CUSTOM Bonded Cargo.



We provide following type of clearances

  • Import Custom Clearance (Air / Sea)
  • Export Custom Clearance (Air / Sea)
  • Project Cargo Clearance
  • Duty Drawback Claim Service
  • Registration / Clearance / Completion of clearances under Duty Exemption Licenses
  • Customs Duty Refund services.
  • Custom Consultancy services

Our Customs Clearing Services include:

  • 1. Guidance and consultancy on pre and post shipment services.
  • 2. Liaison and follow up with various Government Organizations.
  • 3. Documentation procedures i.e. preparation and handling of documents.
  • 4. All post shipment formalities and endorsements.
  • 5. Handling all types of Bill of Entries & Shipping Bills as per the various schemes introduced from time to time by the Government.
